Faris boy needs another 5,000 KM for the fourth operation!


Eleven-year-old Faris broke his arm last year, after which he had two operations in Sarajevo and one in Vienna, for which his parents had borne the costs. His hand literally started to die off but was saved, however it remained dysfunctional. The bone was then transplanted from the hip to the forearm, and it was now planned for the fourth operation to transplant a portion of the bone from the knee to the elbow, and after that surgery the boy could move his arm again. Faris needs about 15,000 KM for the operation. The family has 8,000 KM and Pomozi.ba will pay 2,000 KM from non-earmarked funds, thus missing another 5,000 KM.
